## Data Privacy & Protection
EdStream implements industry-leading security measures to protect student and institutional data:

- **End-to-End Encryption:** All communications encrypted both at rest and in transit
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Granular permissions ensure users only access appropriate data
- **Audit Logging:** Complete activity trails for compliance audits and accountability

## Institutional Control
Your IT team maintains complete control over:
- User authentication via LTI 1.3 and SSO
- Data storage location and retention policies
- Access logs and compliance reporting
- Integration with existing institutional systems

EdStream operates under a **Data Processing Agreement** that ensures your institution's data governance policies are fully respected.

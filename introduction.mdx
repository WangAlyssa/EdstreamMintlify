## Quick Start Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="LMS Authentication (LTI 1.3)">
    Forget about forgotten passwords. EdStream uses enterprise-grade **LTI 1.3** to link directly to your university's **Canvas**, Blackboard, or Moodle instance. Simply click the EdStream link in your course navigation to log in via **Single Sign-On (SSO)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-time Roster Sync">
    Your rosters and project groups sync automatically. If a student is added to a course in the LMS, they are instantly provisioned in the correct EdStream channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the Native Apps">
    Stay connected on the bus or in the lab. Experience native code formatting and push notifications on any device:

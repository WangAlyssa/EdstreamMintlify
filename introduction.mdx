## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="LMS Authentication">
    Forget about forgotten passwords. EdStream uses enterprise-grade **LTI 1.3** to link directly to your university's **Canvas**, Blackboard, or Moodle instance. Simply click the EdStream link in your course navigation to log in via **Single Sign-On (SSO)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Real-time Sync">
    Your rosters and groups sync automatically. If you're added to a course in Canvas, you're in the channel on EdStream instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go Mobile">
    Stay connected on the bus or in the lab. Download our native apps:
    - [Download for iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/edstream/id6736952355)
    - [Download for Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.edstreamchat.app&pcampaignid=web_share)
  </Step>
</Steps>

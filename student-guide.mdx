## Mastering the Request Hub
Academic life happens. If you need a grading review, an extension, or a DRC accommodation, don't send an email. 

<Info>
  Navigate to the **Requests** tab in your sidebar to submit formal inquiries.
</Info>

- **Submission:** Select your assignment, state your reason, and hit submit.
- **Tracking:** You will see a live status badge: **Pending**, **Approved**, or **Denied**.

## Study Without Downloads
Stop cluttering your "Downloads" folder just to check a syllabus. Our platform generates rich, high-quality **inline previews** for Word documents, PowerPoints, and PDFs instantly.

## Your Automated Asset Library
Every photo of a whiteboard, lecture recording link, or handout shared in chat is automatically indexed.
- Open the **Channel Details** sidebar.
- Find the **Media Sorting** area to find any file in seconds.

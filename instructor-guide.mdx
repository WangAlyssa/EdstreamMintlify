## Managing Large Enrollments
Handling 1,000+ students shouldn't be a full-time job. Use **1-Click Channels** to segment your class into Lab Sections or Project Groups automatically.

## One-Click Approvals
Our **Centralized Request** system replaces chaotic student emails.
1. Review reasoning and documents in your unified dashboard.
2. Hit **Approve** or **Deny**.
3. EdStream handles all student notifications automatically.

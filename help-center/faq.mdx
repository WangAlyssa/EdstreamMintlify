## General & Account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is EdStream?" icon="circle-info">
    EdStream is an integrated, real-time messaging and collaboration platform built specifically for higher education and forward-thinking enterprises. It replaces fragmented email threads and legacy LMS forums with a sleek, centralized hub featuring 1-Click Channels, seamless file sharing, and robust administrative tools.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do students need to create separate accounts?" icon="users">
    No. EdStream uses your institution's existing Single Sign-On (SSO) infrastructure. Through our seamless Canvas LTI integration, students and instructors access EdStream directly from their Canvas course navigation without ever needing to remember a separate password or create a new account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Instructors & TAs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I manage extension and grading requests?" icon="inbox">
    EdStream features a built-in **Centralized Request** system. Instead of getting lost in your email inbox, students submit structured requests (e.g., extensions, regrades, attendance issues) directly through the platform. Instructors and TAs can view a unified dashboard of pending requests, approve or deny them with one click, and automatically notify the student.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I control who posts in a channel?" icon="lock">
    Absolutely. With **1-Click Channels**, you have granular control over permissions. You can create read-only announcement channels where only Instructors and TAs can post, private channels for specific project groups, or open channels for general class discussion.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features & Workflow

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Automated Media Sorting work?" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Say goodbye to digging through chat history to find a syllabus. EdStream's Automated Media Sorting intelligently scans channels and automatically categorizes shared assets into designated `Photos`, `Videos`, and `Files` tabs within the channel details pane.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to download files to view them?" icon="eye">
    No. EdStream supports rich inline previews for documents (PDFs, DOCX), images, and videos. You can read a 50-page syllabus or watch a lecture recording directly within the chat interface.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does EdStream support code formatting for computer science courses?" icon="code">
    Yes. EdStream was built with STEM in mind. We feature native syntax highlighting for over 50 programming languages. Simply wrap your code snippets in triple backticks (\`\`\`), and it will render perfectly formatted, making peer-to-peer debugging and TA support infinitely easier.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my institutional data safe?" icon="shield-halved">
    Security is foundational to EdStream. All platform data is secured with end-to-end encryption both at rest and in transit. We are fully compliant with SOC2, GDPR, and FERPA regulations, ensuring that student data remains strictly within institutional control.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Has EdStream been tested at scale?" icon="chart-line-up">
    Yes. EdStream is built for enterprise-grade deployment. In our recent strategic partnership with the University of Florida IT (UF IT), our pilot successfully supported over 5,000 concurrent students without a single minute of downtime, proving our capability to handle massive enrollment courses seamlessly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
